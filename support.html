Frequently Asked Questions

üìñ Getting Started

How do I navigate between books and chapters?

Use the navigation menu at the top of the reading screen to select different books. Within each book, swipe left/right to move between chapters or use the chapter selector dropdown.

How do I bookmark verses?

Long-press on any verse to open the context menu, then select "Add Bookmark." You can add notes, categories, and colors to organize your bookmarks.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! After the initial setup, Bible Study Pro works completely offline. All your notes, bookmarks, and reading progress are saved locally on your device.

üé® Drawing & Annotations

How do I access the drawing tools?

Tap the drawing icon in the toolbar while reading. This opens the drawing overlay where you can select from pen, highlighter, eraser, and other tools.

Can I change colors and brush sizes?

Absolutely! The drawing toolbar includes a color picker and brush size selector. You can choose from preset colors or create custom colors using the color wheel.

Are my drawings saved permanently?

Yes, all drawings and annotations are automatically saved and will appear whenever you return to that passage. With premium sync, they're also backed up to the cloud.

‚≠ê Premium Features

What's included with Premium?

Premium includes cloud sync, unlimited bookmarks and notes, advanced study tools, priority support, and ad-free experience. See the in-app premium section for full details.

How much does Premium cost?

We offer flexible pricing: Monthly ($4.99), Annual ($29.99), and Lifetime ($79.99). All plans include a 7-day free trial so you can try premium features risk-free.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription anytime through your Google Play Store account settings. You'll retain premium features until the end of your billing period.

üîß Troubleshooting

The app is running slowly or crashing

Try these steps: 1) Restart the app, 2) Restart your device, 3) Clear the app cache in Android settings, 4) Update to the latest version. If issues persist, contact support.

My notes or bookmarks disappeared

Check if you're signed into the same account. Local data is tied to your user account. If you have premium sync enabled, your data should restore automatically when you sign in.

Audio/text-to-speech isn't working

Ensure your device volume is up and text-to-speech is enabled in your device's accessibility settings. Some Android devices require additional TTS engine installation.
