Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 8, 2025

At Bible Study Pro, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and password when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Optional profile details you choose to provide
  • User-Generated Content: Notes, bookmarks, annotations, and personal study materials you create

1.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about how you use our app:

  • App Usage: Features used, time spent in app, reading progress
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Performance Data: Crash reports and app performance metrics

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect precise location data
  • We do not access your contacts or other apps
  • We do not collect sensitive personal information beyond what's necessary for app functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To provide core features like saving notes, bookmarks, and reading progress
  • Account Management: To create and maintain your user account
  • Personalization: To customize your app experience and provide relevant content
  • Support: To respond to your questions and provide customer support
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve app features
  • Security: To protect against unauthorized access and maintain app security

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Personal notes and bookmarks
  • Reading progress and preferences
  • Drawing annotations and highlights
  • App settings and configurations

3.2 Cloud Storage (Premium Features)

If you use premium sync features, we may store encrypted copies of your data on secure cloud servers to enable synchronization across devices.

3.3 Security Measures

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data by our team

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

We may share data with trusted service providers who help us operate our app, such as:

  • Cloud storage providers (with encrypted data)
  • Analytics services (anonymized data only)
  • Customer support platforms

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes or government requests
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Investigate potential violations of our terms of service

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

5.1 Access and Control

  • Access: View and export your personal data through the app settings
  • Correction: Update or correct your account information at any time
  • Deletion: Delete your account and associated data
  • Data Portability: Export your notes, bookmarks, and other user-generated content

5.2 Communication Preferences

  • Opt out of promotional emails
  • Disable push notifications in device settings
  • Control which app features can access your data

6. Children's Privacy

Bible Study Pro is designed to be appropriate for users of all ages, including children. We take special care to protect children's privacy:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent
  • Parents can contact us to review, delete, or stop collection of their child's information
  • We encourage parents to supervise their children's use of the app
  • The app does not contain social networking features or user-to-user communication

7. Third-Party Services

7.1 Analytics

We may use third-party analytics services to understand app usage. These services collect anonymized data that cannot be traced back to individual users.

7.2 In-App Purchases

Premium feature purchases are processed through Google Play Store. Google's privacy policy governs the collection and use of payment information.

7.3 External Links

Our app may contain links to external websites or resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites.

8. International Users

Bible Study Pro is available worldwide. If you are located outside the United States:

  • Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States
  • We comply with applicable international privacy laws
  • You have the same privacy rights regardless of your location
  • We provide appropriate safeguards for international data transfers

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Usage Data: Aggregated data may be retained for app improvement purposes
  • Support Data: Customer support interactions retained for resolution and quality purposes
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • For significant changes, we will notify you through the app or by email
  • Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy
  • Previous versions of our Privacy Policy are available upon request

11. Contact Information

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact us:

Email: privacy@biblestudypro.app

Support: support@biblestudypro.app

Developer: Sacred Text Studios

Response Time: We will respond to privacy inquiries within 30 days

12. Your Consent

By using Bible Study Pro, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with these practices, please do not use our app.

Thank you for trusting Bible Study Pro with your Bible study journey. We are committed to protecting your privacy while helping you grow in your faith.

This Privacy Policy is designed to be transparent and comprehensive. If you need clarification on any point, please don't hesitate to contact us.
